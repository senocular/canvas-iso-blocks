Keyboard Shortcuts

← (LEFT):
Move marker left
→ (RIGHT):
Move marker right
↑ (UP):
Move marker up
↓ (DOWN):
Move marker down
CTRL + ← (LEFT):
Spin camera left
CTRL + → (RIGHT):
Spin camera right
CTRL + ↑ (UP):
Tilt camera down
CTRL + ↓ (DOWN):
Tilt camera up
